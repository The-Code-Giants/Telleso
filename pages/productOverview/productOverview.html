Bringing stream-lined social communication solutions to your company. Today

Telleso doesn’t just offer instant chat for employees, although it offers that too

What it offers is an engaging social environment for your employees

It allows employees to be immersed in the company culture, be rewarded, and receive recognition for their work

Creating a Space to Collaborate

Some people say that electronic communication stifles innovation and collaboration. But because Telleso puts social first, it gives employees the perfect environment to collaborate electronically and get things done

Employees can schedule meetings together and work across departments seamlessly. Just as though they were in the office, spurring innovation, new ideas, and problem-solving

Having a social attitude to employee engagement means innovation and collaboration are encouraged. And if employees need some focus, they can work together in groups without distractions

Get The Recognition You Deserve

Employees work wonders. They are the backbone of any organization. With Telleso, it’s easy to reward and acknowledge employees for the hard work they bring to the company

This comes with many benefits. Not only does it create a brilliant company culture where everyone feels valued, it also reduces turnover, and keeps employees happy

Plus, they don’t have to be at their desks in the office to be rewarded. Telleso works wonders if you’re at the office, on the production line, or working remotely

Communication That’s Flexible

Telleso redraws boundaries. It makes communication throughout the company easy for everyone to do.

Yet it also lets you really stay in control of company-wide messaging. With Telleso, you can livestream to the entire company all at once, you could even record that livestream and turn it into a podcast for future employees to hear

Giving your employees the freedom to communicate flexibly on one hand, whilst giving you the clarity of clear communication from the top of the organization

A Depository For Everything

Telleso lets you store company information

Whether that’s podcasts, important company documents that need to be accessible to everyone, or simply internal news articles to keep your employees up to date

It’s much more convenient for everyone and saves so much time.
